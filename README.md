# African Games Collection

Welcome to the official games portfolio of The African Gourmet. This collection brings together interactive experiences that celebrate the rich foodways, symbols, and cultural heritage of the African continent. Each game is a gateway to learning, designed to educate and entertain through play.

## About The African Gourmet

This project is part of The African Gourmet ecosphere, a digital archive dedicated to exploring and preserving African food culture, traditions, and wisdom. Visit [www.theafricangourmet.com](https://www.theafricangourmet.com) to explore the main archive, field notes, recipes, and more.

---

## The Games

### African Foodways: Odd One Out
A rapid-fire puzzle game that tests your knowledge of African dishes. With only 10 seconds per round, you must spot the ingredient or dish that doesn't belong. Inspired by Yoruba wisdom, it is a fun way to learn about the textures, base ingredients, and serving styles of African cuisine.

[Play Odd Out Match Game](https://chicafricanculture.github.io/odd-out-match-game/) | [View Code](https://github.com/ChicAfricanCulture/odd-out-match-game)

---

### The African Pantry Mahjong
Explore the rich foodways of Africa through this classic Mahjong-style matching game. Match ingredients to discover recipes and learn about their cultural significance. With 64 tiles per level, it is a relaxing yet engaging journey through the continent's diverse pantry.

[Play The African Pantry Mahjong](https://chicafricanculture.github.io/The-African-Pantry-Mahjong/) | [View Code](https://github.com/ChicAfricanCulture/The-African-Pantry-Mahjong)

---

### Adinkra Movers
A fast-paced symbol-matching game that brings Adinkra symbols to life. Watch as symbols like "Gye Nyame" move across the screen—tap the golden window when you spot the match. Choose between Normal and Hard modes to test your reflexes while learning the meaning of each sacred symbol.

[Play Adinkra Movers](https://chicafricanculture.github.io/moving-adinkra/) | [View Code](https://github.com/ChicAfricanCulture/moving-adinkra)

---

### Adinkra Mahjong
Match tiles and uncover the wisdom of West Africa's Adinkra symbols. This cultural matching game layers complexity as you progress, with stacked tiles and deep proverbs to guide you. Click any tile to see its meaning and connect with African heritage through play.

[Play Adinkra Mahjong](https://chicafricanculture.github.io/Adinkra-Mahjong/) | [View Code](https://github.com/ChicAfricanCulture/Adinkra-Mahjong)

---

### Taste the Words of Africa
Dive into a letter grid filled with the names of iconic African dishes. From Jollof to Ugali, find and learn about the foods that define a continent. This word puzzle is a delicious challenge for foodies and culture lovers alike.

[Play African Food Word Puzzle](https://chicafricanculture.github.io/african-food-word-puzzle/) | [View Code](https://github.com/ChicAfricanCulture/african-food-word-puzzle)
